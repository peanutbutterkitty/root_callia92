⊹₊⟡⋆Project description⊹₊⟡⋆

We are making a character based on a dragon fruit. The inspiration for the character's appearance is Lolita fashion. Lolita is a subculture from Japan based on the Victorian time period and its clothing. The subculture can primarily be categorized into three sub-styles "Sweet", "Gothic" and "Classic" but they're many more! For our character we chose to do Sweet Lolita because dragon fruits can often be described as sweet when ripe. This project took a lot of patience considering there weren't a lot of tutorials on what we wanted specifically. In the end we did learn a lot about blender and the different tools in the software and we are very proud of our end resault.
